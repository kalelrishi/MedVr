🧠 Brain
The control center of the body, managing all thoughts, movements, and vital functions.
Key Functions: Cognition, coordination, memory, vital control
❤️ Heart
A muscular organ that pumps blood throughout the body.
Key Functions: Circulation, oxygen transport, nutrient delivery
💨 Left Lung
One of two respiratory organs that exchange oxygen and carbon dioxide with blood.
Key Functions: Gas exchange, oxygenation, respiration
💨 Right Lung
One of two respiratory organs that exchange oxygen and carbon dioxide with blood.

🔴 Liver
The largest internal organ, responsible for detoxification and metabolism.
Key Functions: Detoxification, metabolism, bile production
🍽️ Stomach
A muscular organ that breaks down food and mixes it with digestive juices.
Key Functions: Digestion, food breakdown, nutrient processing
🫘 Left Kidney
One of two organs that filter waste from blood to produce urine.
Key Functions: Filtration, waste removal, fluid balance
